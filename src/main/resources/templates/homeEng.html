About Project

The Nexon Foundation wants to create various projects in the areas of IT, culture, play and health.

  • IT Supports the foundation for future generations to grow into creative talents in the field of SW, and discovers and sponsors excellent adolescents with the potential in this field.
  • HEALTH Sponsors the establishment of rehabilitation hospitals and supports long-term self-reliance to ensure that children in need of rehabilitation can receive proper treatment at the right time.
  • CULTURE We introduce various cultural contents to our society and help children and adolescents to access cultural contents more easily.
  • PLAY Play, the right of all children in the world! We are creating a fun play culture that can stimulate children's imagination and creativity using bricks.
