OUR INITIATIVES

  • Technology 1

    Nexon Foundation is building the groundwork for future generations to
    grow into creative talents in the field of software and will continue to
    identify outstanding youths with potential in this very realm.

    See projects

  • Health 1

    Nexon Foundation supports the establishment of rehabilitation hospitals
    and backing long-term self-reliance so that children who are in need of
    rehabilitation can properly receive the right treatment in a timely manner.​

    See projects

  • Culture 3

    Nexon Foundation helps children to gain knowledge and let them
    understand the joy of learning through books. In addition,
    Nexon Foundation introduces various cultural content to our society and
    help children and teenagers to easily access those materials.

    See projects

  • Play 1

    Nexon Foundation helps children around the globe to develop their
    creativity through ‘play’, and nurture them to grow into changemakers.
    Children are invited to the world beyond their imagination via brick,
    the toy that can make anything out of nothing.
