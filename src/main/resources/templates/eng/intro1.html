Introduction

The Nexon Foundation is anon-profit organization establishedby the Nexon Company to spreadsocial contribution activitiescontinuously.

While developing social contribution activities that have been carried out, such as PURME Foundation NEXON Children's Rehabilitation Hospital, which is the Korea's first integrated children’s rehabilitation hospital, NYPC, which is the coding contest for teenagers, Brick donation for spreading creative play culture including Nexon small bookstore program started in 2005, we are promoting new projects for future generations in the areas of IT, culture, play and health under the motto of ‘From a CHILD’.
