PLAY

Play, the right of all children in the world!
We are creating a fun play culture that can stimulate children's imagination and creativity using bricks.

Dream in Brick

It is a project that develops and supports programs stimulating children's imagination and creativity through bricks, which are toys that allow children to make anything to spread the creative play culture. In 2018, we donated 4.9 million bricks to Myanmar, Cambodia, Nepal, and Mongolia as well as our local children's centers, and we will find various NGO partners and build partnerships to provide creative play opportunities to children around the world.
