Small bookstore

Starting with the first store in Punghwa Branch School in Tongyeong-si, Gyeongnam in 2004, NEXON Small Bookstore is a representative social contribution project of NEXSON Company that provides the place of knowledge and learning for children to access the books in easy and entertaining ways. A total of 119 bookstores are operated including 112 in Korea, 7 in foreign countries such as Burundi, Nepal, Cambodia, Laos, and over 100,000 books have been donated. In addition, they are used as reading places and various cultural experience spaces for about 100,000 children around the world.
